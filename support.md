# Reciplease Support

Welcome to Reciplease! This guide will help you get the most out of your cooking companion.

## Getting Started

### First Time Using Reciplease

1. **Scan Your Ingredients**: Take photos of your fridge, pantry, or ingredients
2. **Review Your Pantry**: Check and edit the detected ingredients
3. **Generate Recipes**: Get personalized recipe suggestions based on what you have
4. **Cook & Enjoy**: Follow the recipes and favorite the ones you love!

### Quick Start Guide

**Step 1: Scan Ingredients**
- Tap "Start Scanning" on the home screen
- Take up to 4 photos of your ingredients (you can use your camera or photo library)
- Tap "Scan Ingredients" to process your photos
- The app will automatically detect ingredients from labels, packaging, and produce

**Step 2: Review Your Pantry**
- Check the detected ingredients in the Ingredients tab
- Edit ingredient names, quantities, or remove items as needed
- Your pantry is saved automatically

**Step 3: Generate Recipes**
- Tap "Generate Recipes" from the home screen or recipes tab
- Optionally add preferences like "vegetarian", "quick meals", or dietary restrictions
- Browse your personalized recipe suggestions

**Step 4: Explore Recipes**
- Tap any recipe to view full details including ingredients, instructions, prep time, and difficulty
- Generate AI images for recipes by tapping the image area
- Favorite recipes you love for easy access later

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I scan ingredients?
1. Tap "Start Scanning" on the home screen
2. Take photos with your camera or select from your photo library
3. You can add up to 4 photos at once
4. Tap "Scan Ingredients" to process
5. Review and edit the detected ingredients

### How many photos can I scan at once?
You can scan up to 4 photos per scan session. This helps ensure accurate ingredient detection.

### Can I edit detected ingredients?
Yes! After scanning, you can:
- Edit ingredient names
- Add or modify quantities
- Remove ingredients you don't have
- Manually add ingredients

### How do I generate recipes?
1. Make sure you have ingredients in your pantry
2. Tap "Generate Recipes" from the home screen or recipes tab
3. Optionally add preferences (e.g., "vegetarian", "quick meals")
4. Tap to generate personalized recipes

### Can I customize recipes?
Yes! While viewing a recipe, you can modify it with requests like:
- "Make it vegetarian"
- "Reduce cooking time to 30 minutes"
- "Add more spice"
- "Make it gluten-free"

### How do I favorite a recipe?
While viewing a recipe, tap the star/favorite button. Favorited recipes appear in the Favorites tab.

### What happens to my data?
Your pantry, recipes, and favorites are stored locally on your device. We also use your data to personalize your experience and improve our services. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

### How do I restore my subscription?
1. Open the Settings screen
2. Tap "Subscription" or the crown icon
3. Tap "Restore Purchases"
4. Sign in with the Apple ID you used to purchase

### Can I use the app offline?
Yes! After initial setup, most features work offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to:
- Scan new ingredients
- Generate new recipes
- Generate recipe images
- Modify recipes

## Troubleshooting

### Scanning Issues

**Ingredients not detected correctly**
- Ensure photos are well-lit and in focus
- Make sure ingredient labels are visible
- Try taking photos from different angles
- You can manually edit or add ingredients after scanning

**Scan is taking too long**
- Check your internet connection
- Processing typically takes 10-40 seconds depending on the number of photos
- Try closing and reopening the app if it seems stuck

### Recipe Generation Issues

**No recipes generated**
- Make sure you have ingredients in your pantry
- Check your internet connection
- Try again - sometimes the service needs a moment

**Recipes don't match my preferences**
- Try being more specific in your preferences
- You can modify recipes after generation
- Generate multiple times to get different suggestions

### Subscription Issues

**Can't access Pro features**
- Make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
- Try restoring purchases from Settings
- Check that your subscription is active in your Apple account

**Payment issues**
- Contact Apple Support for billing questions
- Check your Apple ID payment method
- Verify your subscription status in App Store settings

### General Issues

**App crashes or freezes**
- Close and reopen the app
- Restart your device
- Make sure you're running the latest version
- Contact support if the issue persists

**Data not saving**
- Make sure you have storage space available
- Check that you're not in airplane mode
- Try closing and reopening the app
